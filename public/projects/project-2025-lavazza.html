Lavazza Fortuna

Interactive Wall Design, Lavazza Professionals Office, Verden/Bremen, 2025

“Inspiriert von der Tradition des Kaffeesatzlesens erzählt die Lavazza Fortuna Wall die Geschichten, die mit jeder Tasse entstehen.”

The aim of this project is to transform everyday coffee breaks in the office into small moments of interaction and engagement.

Inspired by the tradition of reading fortunes from Turkish coffee, Lavazza Fortuna Wall reinterprets this ritual as a contemporary spatial experience. A simple physical action is translated into a digital response, creating an intuitive interaction between user and installation.

The system is based on a heat sensor embedded in the wall. When a hot coffee cup is brought close to the surface, the sensor detects the temperature and activates the system. One of Lavazza’s twelve core values is then randomly selected and displayed together with a short, poetic message.

Connessioni — A cup of coffee

A cup of coffee connects hearts. The most meaningful connections often happen in simple, everyday moments. Over a cup of coffee, words can find their way more easily.

Connessioni — Eine Tasse Kaffee

Eine Tasse Kaffee verbindet Herzen. Die schönsten Verbindungen entstehen oft ganz nebenbei – bei einem einfachen Kaffee. In ihrer Wärme finden Worte den Mut, ausgesprochen zu werden.

By combining a familiar ritual with sensor-based interaction, Lavazza Fortuna Wall adds a new layer to a routine action and introduces a subtle moment of connection within the workspace.
